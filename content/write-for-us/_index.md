## Become a Contributor

AppliedAM is building a publication for additive manufacturing — where
inventors, manufacturers, and practitioners share real work and insight.

Beyond featured Research, Applications, and Case Studies, we also publish
general articles on AM trends, industry commentary, explainers, and
opinion pieces. If you have expertise and a perspective worth sharing,
we want to hear from you.

---

## What We Publish

- **Industry commentary** — analysis of trends, technologies, and shifts in AM
- **Explainers** — accessible breakdowns of processes, materials, or standards
- **Opinion** — informed perspectives on where AM is heading
- **Interviews and profiles** — conversations with people building in AM

---

## Why Contribute

- **Reach** — published alongside featured research and industry case studies
- **Byline** — full author credit and bio on every piece
- **No paywalls** — open access publication, built for visibility
- **Ongoing** — contributors can write regularly, not just once

---

## How to Apply

Send the following to **writers@appliedam.net**:

1. A short bio and background
2. A writing sample (published or unpublished)
3. 2-3 article ideas or topics
4. Links — LinkedIn, portfolio, or previous work (optional)
