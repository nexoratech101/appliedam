About AppliedAM

Where AM Knowledge Gets Published

AppliedAM is an independent publication for the additive manufacturing community. We publish original research, industry applications, and real-world case studies from the people actually doing the work.

Our goal is straightforward - give inventors, manufacturers, and practitioners a credible place to share what they have learned, and give the wider AM community a reliable source of practical knowledge.
